About the Game

I worked on the Singstar franchise in my time at Sony Playstation. As a creative designer I worked on both the UI develoment and the marketing side of the franchise.

My Task

I worked on the Singstar franchise in my time at Sony Playstation. As a creative designer I worked closely with the senior design team and was tasked with creating a variety of marketplace imagery used to promote both content available to the end user and to indicate upcming tiltes that would be released soon. I also worked on the Global Playstation Store creating marketing messages to promote the game updates to potential customers.
I also had to oppertunity to work on a selection of in-game assets including iconography, screen lay-outs and having the oppertunity to present work back to the senior memebers of the team.

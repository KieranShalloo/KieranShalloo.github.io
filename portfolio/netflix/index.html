Netflix on YouView

Christmas Advertising Campaign

Brief

I was tasked with creating a short animation to promote the arrival of the Netflix service on the YouView platform. I initially worked with a contracted motion designer to create the initial vision although i personally took the project through from that point to final completion. There was a lot of communication from not only the internal team but also with the Netflix team to ensure that it promoted the platform. This was seen across Londonâ€™s Piccadilly Circus during the peak christmas period

Client

Netflix & YouView TV Ltd

Project Date

2015

Role

Motion Graphics, Animation, Art Direction

The Goal

Sony and YouView worked on creating a collaborative project that brought the front end of the YouView platform to Android TV. Working alongside a team of contracted developers we wanted to not just port the YouView UI but to create a integrated bespoke system which leverages the power of the Google back-end. This collaboration would lead to YouView being the base of UK television viewing on Sony released TVs

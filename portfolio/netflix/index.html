Netflix on YouView

Christmas Advertising Campaign

Brief

I was tasked with creating a short animation to promote the arrival of the Netflix service on the YouView platform. I initially worked with a contracted motion designer to create the initial vision although i personally took the project through from that point to final completion. There was a lot of communication from not only the internal team but also with the Netflix team to ensure that it promoted the platform. This was seen across London’s Piccadilly Circus during the peak christmas period

Client

Netflix & YouView TV Ltd

Target Audience

Customers who currently use the service but may have difficulty using the product due to sight-related issues. Customers who wish to have a more accessible TV service

Project Date

2015

Focus

The legacy YouView help menu was built as a way for customers to solve the problems they were facing in their own time. The depreciation of the legacy UI and the move to the Next Generation interface pushed forward the need to bring the Help App into line with the revamped service.

I was chosen to lead this project working alongside a junior designer and an intern, helping to guide them towards a final product

Role

Motion Graphics, Animation, Art Direction

The Goal

Sony and YouView worked on creating a collaborative project that brought the front end of the YouView platform to Android TV. Working alongside a team of contracted developers we wanted to not just port the YouView UI but to create a integrated bespoke system which leverages the power of the Google back-end. This collaboration would lead to YouView being the base of UK television viewing on Sony released TVs

Star Wars: One Careful Owner

Inspired by the first Star Wars film "A New Hope" This print aimed to give a fresh approach on one of the worlds most loved franchise. I wanted to think aout what may have happened in Star Wars off screen when the camera was following the films characters elsewhere. It was mentioned in a throw away line that Luke Skywalker sold his Landspeeder to help fund the journey. Well now you can see it.
